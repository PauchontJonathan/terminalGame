Terminal-Game

Music by Joystock - https://www.joystock.org

Welcome in the terminal-game. In this game, you will need to help the character to find his way out to the flag. To do that, you have a terminal interface. Try tapping "/help" there to learn how it works. You'll need to answer to different kind of questions, if you have more than three errors on one question then you will restart to the beginning ! Good Luck !
